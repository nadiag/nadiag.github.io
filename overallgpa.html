How to calculate overall GPA

Everything you need to know to calculate your overall GPA.

Follow these steps to calculate your overall GPA.

  1. Click the link to the 'Grades Menu' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Click the 'Overall Grade Point Average (GPA) Calculator ' link in the menu.
  3. Enter in the GPA you need at the end of the semester into the text box beside 'Overall GPA Needed at end of semester.'
    Note: There is a green arrow and statement beside the box that says: change this.
  4. Click the 'Calculate' button.
    Note: You may change the total hours and credits to calculate different scenarios.

How to do Course Request

Everything you need to know to complete Course Request.

Follow these steps to complete Course Request.

  1. Click the link to 'Registration and Schedule' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and locate the table titled 'Registration Links.'
  3. Click on the 'Course Request' link for the term that you are requesting classes for.
  4. Type the Course Request Number (CRN) for the classes that you are requesting into the available text boxes and click 'Submit.'
    Note: The CRN is found on the Timetable of classes.
  5. View the results when course request is returned. There will be a link to 'Course Requst Results' that you will click on (located in the same location as the 'Course Request' link). This will show you what classes you did and did not recieve.

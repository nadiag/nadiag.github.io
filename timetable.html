How to access the Timetable of Classes

Everything you need to know to access the Timetable of classes.

Follow these steps to access the Timetable.

  1. Click the link to 'Registration and Schedule' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and locate the table titled 'Registration Links.'
  3. Click on the 'Timetable of Classes' link for the term that you are looking up classes for.
  4. Select the subject of the class from the drop-down menu under the Subject heading.
  5. Type in the Course Number or the Course Request Number (CRN) for the specific course if you have that information available.
  6. Find the course you are searching for in the Timetable.
  7. Make note of the time that the course is held, the professor who is teaching the course, and the CRN Number.

How to complete the housing contract

Everything you need to know to complete your housing contract.

Follow these steps to complete the housing contract.

  1. Click the link to 'Housing and Dining Services' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Click the 'Complete the Housing Contract' link in the menu.
  3. Follow the instructions on the page.
    Note: This page will only display while Virginia Tech is still accepting applications to live on-campus.

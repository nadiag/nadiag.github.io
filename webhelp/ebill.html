How to view and pay the e-bill

Everything you need to know to view and pay the e-bill.

Follow these steps to view and pay the e-bill.
  1. Click the link to the 'University Account Information' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Click the 'View and Pay e-bill' link in the menu.
  3. Click the 'Make Payment.' link on the sidebar.
  4. Enter the amount you would like to pay and select the method from the drop-down menu and click continue.
    Note: You may view previous transactions under the 'Transaction History' link on the sidebar.
  5. Follow the instructions and enter all of your payment information.

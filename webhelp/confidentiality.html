How to change confidentiality settings

Everything you need to know to change your confidentiality settings.

Follow these steps to change your confidentiality settings.

  1. Click the link to the 'Personal Information' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Click the 'Confidentiality Options' link from the menu.
  3. Check the boxes next to the listed information that you would like to make confidential.
  4. Click 'Submit Changes.'

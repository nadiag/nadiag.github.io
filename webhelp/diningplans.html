How to modify dining plans

Everything you need to know to modify your dining plan.

Follow these steps to modify your dining plan.

  1. Click the link to 'Housing and Dining Services' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Click the 'Add/Change Individual Dining Plan' link in the menu.
  3. Select the term you want to change the dining plan for from the drop-down menu and click 'Submit.'
  4. Select your desired dining plan.
    Note: Virginia Tech's student dining plans can be viewed here: http://www.dining.vt.edu/dining-plans_policies/major_plans.html
  5. Click 'Submit.'

Hokiespa Instructions Manual

Grade and Degree Menus

This section covers two aspects of HokieSpa: the Grades Menu and the Degree Menu. As you might expect, the Grades Menu is where all your grade-related information will be posted; you'll be able to view your midterm grades (for freshmen) and final grades, as well as use a variety of tools for calculating and predicting your GPA and a record of transfer and additional credits you have received. In the Degree Menu, we will explain how to use the Degree Audit Report, a valuable tool for keeping track of your academic progress that many don't know how to use.

Registration and Schedule

The Registration and Schedule menu will most likely be the section of Hokie SPA you use the most. Registration and Schedule contains all of the materials and information necessary for class registration and graduation. Within this section, you will learn how to navigate the Timetable of Classes and sign up for classes, using both Course Request and Drop/Add. These are tasks that will be important and frequent for the duration of your enrollment at Virginia Tech.

How to view the DARS report

Everything you need to know to view the DARS report.

Follow these steps to view the DARS report.

  1. Click the link to the 'Degree Menu' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Click the 'Undergraduate Student Degree Menu' link in the menu.
  3. Click the link for 'Degree Audit Report Menu.'
  4. Click the link for 'Request a Degree Audit Report.'
  5. Choose whether you would like complete or incomplete requirements.
    Note: Complete requirements displays only classes you have completed, incomplete requirements displays remaining requirements as well.
  6. Select 'Yes' or 'No' to whether you would like to use planned courses.
  7. Select 'Run Audit.'
  8. Click 'View Submitted Audits', and wait until the audit becomes available.
    Note: Complete requirements will display in green, and incomplete requirements will display in red.

How to access Hokiespa

Everything you need to know to access Hokiespa.

Follow these steps to access Hokiespa.

  1. Go to https://banweb.banner.vt.edu/ssb/prod/twbkwbis.P_WWWLogin.
  2. Click 'Login to Hokiespa'
  3. Enter your Virginia Tech PID and password and click 'login'.
  4. Click the link to 'Hokiespa' on the menu.

How to Drop/Add a Class

Everything you need to know to drop/add a class.

Follow these steps to drop/add a class.

  1. Click the link to the 'Registration and Schedule' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and locate the table titled 'Registration Links.'
  3. Click on the 'Drop/Add Classes' link for the term that you are requesting classes for.
  4. Select 'Yes' from the drop-down menu located beside the class to drop the class.
  5. Enter the Course Request Number (CRN) in the available text boxes below the heading 'CRNs' to add a class.
    Note: The CRN is found on the Timetable of classes.
  6. Make sure to click 'Submit Changes' at the bottom of the screen when you are finished.

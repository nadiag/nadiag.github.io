How to view room assignments

Everything you need to know to view your room assignment.

Follow these steps to view your room assignment.

  1. Click the link to 'Housing and Dining Services' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Click the 'View Room Assignment' link in the menu.
    Note: Dorm and room assignments will be displayed here.

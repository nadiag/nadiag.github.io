Personal Information

The Personal Information page is where you can manage how the university contacts you. From here, you'll be able to change your listed addresses and phone numbers, as well as your emergency contacts. Additionally, you'll have the choice of what information you want to keep confidential, meaning it can't be viewed by third-parties.

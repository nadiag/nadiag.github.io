How to view final grades

Everything you need to know to view final grades.

Follow these steps to view your final grades.

  1. Click the link to the 'Grades Menu' sub-menu from the Hokiespa menu.
  2. Click the 'Final Grades' link in the menu.
  3. Select the term you would like to view final grades from in the drop-down menu.
  4. Click submit and your final grades will be displayed.
    Note: Your grades for the current semester, your GPA for the current semester, and your overall GPA will be displayed in tables.
